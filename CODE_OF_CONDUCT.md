# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Personal AI OS to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project for people building local-first AI systems.

Contributors are expected to communicate with professionalism, assume good intent, and keep technical discussion focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Prefer clear technical reasoning over personal criticism.
- Welcome questions from new contributors.
- Keep discussions relevant to project goals, reliability, safety, and maintainability.
- Avoid sharing private data, credentials, or sensitive logs in public channels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, exploit details, or sensitive user data in public issues or pull requests.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after maintainers ask to refocus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to temporary or permanent restrictions from project spaces.
